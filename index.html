Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does laser hair removal work on all hair colors?

Laser hair removal works best on darker hair since the laser targets pigment. It is less effective on white, gray, red, or very light blonde hair.

  • How many sessions will I need?

Most clients need between 6 to 8 sessions to see full results, spaced about 4–6 weeks apart. The exact number may vary depending on the area and your unique hair growth cycle.

  • Is there any downtime?

No. You can return to your normal activities immediately after your appointment.

  • Is laser hair removal permanent?

Laser hair removal offers long-term hair reduction. While some clients may need occasional maintenance sessions, the vast majority experience lasting smoothness and a significant reduction in hair growth.
